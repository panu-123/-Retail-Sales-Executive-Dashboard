# 📊 Retail Sales Executive Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI** dashboard designed to analyze retail sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends. The dashboard transforms raw retail data into actionable business insights using **Power Query**, **DAX**, and interactive visualizations.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Retail Sales Executive Dashboard** provides a comprehensive view of retail business performance through dynamic KPIs, sales trends, regional analysis, product performance, and payment mode insights. It enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Monitor overall retail business performance.
- Analyze sales and profit trends over time.
- Compare regional sales performance.
- Identify top-performing product categories.
- Analyze customer purchasing patterns.
- Evaluate payment mode distribution.
- Support strategic business decisions through interactive reporting.
